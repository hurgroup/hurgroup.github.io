About HUR Group

Who we are

HUR group started in September 2014 when Dr. Pilwon Hur started his position as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Texas A&M University. The acronym "HUR" has several meanings: 1) HUman Rehabilitation, 2) Humans Union Robots, 3) Humans, You (U), and Robots, and 4) the last name of Dr. Hur.

HUR group ultimately seeks to 1) understand how the central nervous system controls human sensorimotor behavior in Bayesian optimal ways, 2) develop and control robots that autonomously and seamlessly interact with humans for muliple purposes, e.g., assistance, rehabilitation. Keywords

The keywords for HUR group include robotics, human-robot interaction, wearable robotics, dynamics, nonlinear control, optimal/robust control, impedance control, gait and balance, state/intent recognition via Bayesian estimation and machine learning, neuromechanics, free energy principle, human motor control, rehabilitation, sensory augmentation
