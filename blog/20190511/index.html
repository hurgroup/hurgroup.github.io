[2019/5/11] Drs. Kenneth Chao and Yitsen Pan graduated!

May 11th, 2019, both Kenneth and Yitsen graduated. Kenneth and Yitsen joined HurGroup as PhD students in 2015 and 2014, respectively. Now, Kenneth is working as a research engineer in Flexiv Robotics, Santa Clara, CA, and Yitsen is working as a postdoctoral researcher at Georgia Tech.

Congratulations for your achievement and graduation.
