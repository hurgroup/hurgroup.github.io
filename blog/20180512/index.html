[2018/5/12] OPTech 2018

Written by Pilwon Hur (2018/5/12)

On 5/12/2018, I gave a talk to 2018 Orthotic AND Prosthetic Innovative Technologies Conference (OPTech). OPTech is hosted and presented by the UCSF Department of Orthopaedic Surgery, Division of Orthotics and Prosthetics. This conference aims to foster emerging technologies that can advance patient care and have a transformative impact on the field of orthotics and prosthetics.

The audiences are physical therapists, orthotists and prosthetists. Invited speakers include physicians, engineers (like me or Dr. Collins), neuroscientists, CEOs of O&P industries.

Please refer to the program.

http://optech.ucsf.edu/
