[2017/3/10] Ivan, an Undergraduate Intern

Written by Pilwon Hur (2017/3/10)

Ivan Gutierrez Arias visited our group for a brief intern in Spring 2017. Ivan is a senior student from Pontificia Universidad Catolica De Chile. Ivan and Seungjun worked on experimental validation of the biomechanical model of assistive glove for stroke patients. Ivan stayed from 1/14/2017 to 3/10/2017. We will miss you Ivan.
