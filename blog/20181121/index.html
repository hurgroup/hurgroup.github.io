[2018/11/21] Fire during thanksgiving break

Written by Pilwon Hur (2018/11/26)

During the Thanksgiving break, there was a fire in the 3D printing lab that is right next to our lab. Even though our lab was not directly damaged by the fire, the lab was messed up with the fume and smoke. Also, soots were all over the surface in the lab. It's all recovered at this moment.
