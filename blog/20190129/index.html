[2019/1/29] Prosthetic Research Highlighted

Written by Pilwon Hur (2019/1/29)

Our new powered transfemoral prosthesis was highlighted. This design is based on Dan McGowan's work and we got a provisional patent and an international PCT patent on this. We are currently working with two famous companies on possible licensing and collaboration.
