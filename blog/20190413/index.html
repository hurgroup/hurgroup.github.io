[2019/4/13] Christian received a best paper award from SCASB

Written by Pilwon Hur (2019/4/13)

On 4/13/2019, Christian received the best paper award for his research titled ***"Support for the Existence of Open-Loop and Closed-Loop Regions in Balance Control"*** at South Central Americal Society of Biomechanics (SCASB). He also got a monetary prize. Congratulations!

SCASB was held 4/12-13 this year at Texas Back Institute at Plano, TX. This year, Woolim, Moein, and Christian presented their work. In SCASB 2017, Moein's work was selected as the best paper award.
