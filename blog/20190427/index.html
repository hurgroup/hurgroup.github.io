[2019/4/27] TURTLE Hatchling Competition

Written by Pilwon Hur (2019/4/27)

There was a TURTLE Robotics Hatchling competition in MEOB 301 on April 27th, 2019 from 10 AM till 12 PM noon. During the meeting, all teams updated and showcased their projects. Also, new students did the Hatchling competition. Dr. Hur is the advisor of TURTLE. TURTLE means Texas A&M University Robotics Team and Leadership Experience.
