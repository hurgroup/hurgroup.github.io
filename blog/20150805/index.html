[2015/8/5] TASB 2015 and Road Trip

Written by Pilwon Hur (2015/8/9)

Hur group (Pilwon, Hyojin, Han, Moein, Kenneth, Yitsen, Dongil) attended ASB 2015 at Columbus, OH from 8/5-8/8. Since we decided to drive from College Station to Columbus, we departed one day earlier. We started 6:30 am on 8/4, had a dinner a Memphis TN and stayed a night at Nashville TN. We returned on 8/8, had a dinner at Louisville KS, and stayed a night at Little Rock AR.

The conference was very useful, and we learned many interesting research idea and trends. We also enjoyed many delicious foods and beautiful scenes.

Next year is Raleigh NC. We may fly, luckily.
